# Liwonde Sun Hotel Digital Platform
## Client-Friendly Website and Admin Guide

Welcome to your complete hotel digital platform.

This system combines a beautiful guest-facing website with a powerful admin control center, so your team can market the property, take bookings, manage operations, and review business performance in one place.

---

## What You Now Have

### 1) A full public website for guests

Your guests can:

- Discover your hotel through modern pages and galleries
- View rooms and amenities
- Check availability
- Make bookings
- Explore conference, events, restaurant, and gym offerings
- Submit reviews

This means your website is not just informational. It drives real bookings and supports your brand image.

### 2) A full admin system for your team

Your staff can:

- Manage bookings and check-ins
- Manage rooms and blocked dates
- Handle payments and invoices
- Manage content (pages, galleries, menus, themes)
- Track maintenance activities
- View reports and export data
- Manage users and internal access

Everything is centralized so daily hotel operations are easier and more organized.

---

## Daily Team Workflow (Simple)

A practical day-to-day flow is:

1. Open dashboard and review arrivals/departures
2. Process new bookings and check-ins
3. Handle room availability and maintenance needs
4. Confirm payments and invoices
5. Update website content when needed
6. Run reports for management decisions

## Business Value for Liwonde Sun Hotel

This platform helps you:

- Increase direct bookings
- Reduce manual coordination across departments
- Improve guest experience and response speed
- Keep records organized for management and finance
- Make faster, data-driven decisions using reports
