Delivery Boundaries

Out of Scope (Expectation Boundaries)

To align expectations clearly, this delivery is a website support and management platform. It is not a full enterprise replacement of all existing internal systems.

  • Not a full ERP replacement for all finance, procurement, and HR processes
  • Not a full migration/rebuild of every legacy system currently in use
  • Not a multi-property enterprise suite with centralized cross-property controls
  • Not a complete replacement for any highly specialized third-party systems already adopted

This scope was intentionally designed to deliver strong operational value at practical cost, while keeping implementation focused on website and day-to-day hotel management support.
