What Can Be Done From Admin Side (Full)

Reservations and Front Office

  • Create, update, and track bookings from one dashboard
  • View booking details and process guest check-ins
  • Handle tentative bookings and convert them to confirmed stays
  • Manage room blocked dates for operational planning

Rooms and Maintenance Control

  • Manage room inventory and room setup details
  • Schedule and track maintenance tasks
  • Assign maintenance to exact room units where applicable
  • Synchronize maintenance impact with room/date availability

Finance and Revenue Management

  • Record and manage payments
  • Create and review invoice information
  • Monitor accounting summaries and payment status
  • Use financial and performance reports for decision-making

Website, Content, and Marketing Management

  • Edit website pages and key section content without code changes
  • Manage image galleries and media content
  • Manage menu items and hospitality content updates
  • Control section headers, themes, and branded presentation
  • Manage events, conference information, gym content, and restaurant flows

Guest Engagement and Service Quality

  • Review and manage guest reviews
  • Track guest-facing interactions tied to operational modules
  • Improve service quality using review and activity feedback

Users, Access, and Administration

  • Manage users and employee records
  • Control secure login, password reset, and account access
  • Apply role-based access patterns for operational safety
  • Track administrative actions for accountability

Analytics and Reporting

  • Open visual reports for operational and management insights
  • Export report data to CSV for finance and leadership review
  • Review activity logs for audit and governance checks
  • Use maintenance and booking reporting to support planning

Daily Operations Workflow

  1. Open dashboard and review arrivals/departures.
  2. Process new bookings and check-ins.
  3. Manage room availability and maintenance tasks.
  4. Track payments and invoice status.
  5. Update site content (pages/gallery/menu) when needed.
  6. Export reports for management review.

Weekly Management Workflow

  1. Review occupancy, booking trends, and revenue movement.
  2. Audit maintenance actions and room availability impact.
  3. Review guest reviews and service quality feedback.
  4. Export reports for leadership planning and finance checks.
